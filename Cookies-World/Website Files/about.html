About

Cookie World is an ecommerce website dedicated to promoting deserts and drinks from café joints located near University Putra Malaysia. It implements a pre-order system to smoothen the ordering and queuing process; decreasing congestion in store and hosting a review system to offer insights to customers regarding the quality of the food items being sold.

For customers, Cookie World serves as a centralized hub of information for multiple joints. Allowing customers to locate up to date information on a variety of hosted cafés. Furthermore, Cookie World which serves as an ecommerce website allows the pre-ordering of food which can later be picked up by the customer. Customers may also leave reviews and feedback on the Cookie World website.

For café and joints, Cookie World serve as an advertising platform that aims to increase traffic to stores while providing a reliable and up to date information hub alongside the joints pre-existing social media account. Cookie World also minimizes the traffic and smooths the purchasing process by allowing pre-orders of the joints products.
